George Brown College (GBC) is a public college of applied arts and technology in Toronto, Canada. 
Founded in 1967, the college offers a wide range of diploma, degree, and postgraduate programs in various fields, 
including business, technology, design, health sciences, and hospitality.
GBC is known for its industry-focused curriculum, providing students with hands-on experience through co-op programs 
and work-integrated learning opportunities. With campuses in downtown Toronto, the college offers access to a diverse 
and dynamic learning environment.
